v1.6.1

Fiverr Alert just got better

Your Fiverr tab now shows a red icon and can chime the moment a new message or notification arrives — so you never miss a buyer again.

What's new

  • Works with the latest Fiverr. Notification detection was rebuilt for Fiverr's redesigned header, so the red icon is reliable again.
  • Sound alerts. An optional chime when a new message or notification lands. Turn it on or off and set the volume from the toolbar popup.
  • Lighter and future-proof. Rebuilt on Manifest V3 and dropped jQuery — smaller, faster, and ready for current Chrome.

How to use it

  1. Click the Fiverr Alert icon in your toolbar to open the settings.
  2. Toggle the notification sound and set the volume to your liking.
  3. Heads up: browsers only allow sound after you've clicked the page once, so the first chime after a reload plays on your next click.
